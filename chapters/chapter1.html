Chapter 1: How We Met

January 15, 2023

FISH camp brought me you, and for that I'm forever grateful. Even though you might not believe it, I noticed how pretty you were the first time I saw you. I thought you were funny and outgoing. I wanted to be friends with you from the jump. It took a while, but we got there eventually.

I loved when I started to catch on to your playing dumb and playing up how much you couldn't hear to make others laugh. The idiots thought that you were actually dumb, but I told you from the start I was on to you. I knew you were smart as an owl. I knew from then that I wanted to be your friend.
